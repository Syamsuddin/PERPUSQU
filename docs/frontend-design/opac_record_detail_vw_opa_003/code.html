By Prof. Elena Vance & Dr. Marcus Aris

Call Number

description Abstract

"Architecture of Light" explores the intersection of spatial design and photons. This comprehensive record details the psychological impact of natural illumination within public institutional spaces, providing a blueprint for the next generation of sustainable campus infrastructure. Professor Vance presents over 200 case studies from the last decade of global architectural evolution.
